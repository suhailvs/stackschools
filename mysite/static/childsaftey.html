Child Safety and Protection Policy

Effective date: 5/10/2025

This page describes the standards and practices of letsystem regarding child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). This policy is publicly available and applies to all users of our platform.

1. Zero-tolerance policy

We maintain a zero-tolerance approach toward any content, behaviour, or use of our platform that involves or promotes child sexual abuse, exploitation, or endangerment.

  • All content that depicts, describes, or sexualizes minors is strictly prohibited.
  • Users must not attempt to solicit sexual activity from minors or facilitate such behaviour.
  • Links to external CSAM or exploitative material are prohibited.

2. Reporting and takedown

We provide the following reporting channels for suspected CSAE:

  • In-app reporting feature:
  • Email reports to: suhailvs@gmail.com

Reports are reviewed immediately upon receipt. Confirmed violating content will be removed without delay and preserved for possible law enforcement requests.

3. Enforcement

When a user is found to violate this policy, we will take appropriate actions, which may include:

  • Immediate removal of the offending content.
  • Temporary or permanent suspension of user accounts.
  • Retention of records and evidence for a reasonable period to support investigations.
  • Referral to and cooperation with law enforcement and child protection agencies where required by law.

4. Cooperation with authorities

We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations. Where necessary and permitted by law, we will provide information to assist investigations.

5. Prevention and education

We are committed to educating our community about online safety and the signs of abuse. We encourage users to report any suspicious behaviour and provide resources for safe online practices.

How to contact us

If you encounter content or behaviour that violates this policy, please report it immediately:

  • Email: suhailvs@gmail.com

We take every report seriously and aim to respond within 24–72 hours.
